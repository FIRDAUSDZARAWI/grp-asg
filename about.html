About Us




Located at Pavilion Kuala Lumpur, The Obsessed Salon provides you with a range of highly trendy haircuts, styling, hair colouring, and rebonding, keratin treatment from a team of highly skilled and experienced hair stylists.




We celebrate women in their most raw, authentic brilliance, who believe in themselves and are ready to step into the look they love. Teamwork is our most valuable asset, which ensures our clients are always number one. At the Obsessed Salon, we strive to exceed your expectations.

Motto

To serve customers with integrity by being loyal to them, the company and ourselves. To give superior customer service consistently with happy relentless attitude while standing within an affordable budget for families.
