Services Details

1. Healthy Scalp Treatment

  • You should definitely consider adding a scalp treatment to your express salon services. This can be as simple as using clarifying shampoos to lift buildup from the roots and massaging a good conditioner into their scalp.
  • This can help stimulate cells in the scalp and increase circulation. Clients will leave feeling fresher, more renewed, and with a healthier head of hair at little to no extra work from you.

2. Express Colour Treatments for Every Hair Type

  • You can add different express colour treatments to your salon services. Offering a demi-permanent colour blend to people with grey hair can help them cover greys that have emerged after their last colouring procedure.
  • Some individuals may only want to touch up their roots or add highlights or lowlights. Your clients will feel more assured about their colour if you offer quick colour touch-ups for all hair types.

3. Hair Styling Services

  • Weddings have never been so well captured for public consumption—again, thanks to social media. Profit from this by giving brides a look they can't wait to share on Instagram, tagging your salon and stylists in the process.

4. Volumizing Hair Treatments

  • Flat hair is a common hair problem for many women, especially those with fine hair. No amount of teasing, hair spray, or hot rollers can help boost their roots. Adding an express service for extra volume would be an easy sell to those suffering from flat hair.
